Rural Communities Can Grow and Thrive

Water is essential to everything we do here in rural America.
Farmers, ranchers, small business owners, schools, health clinics, families – we all need reliable access to safe, clean water.

At CoBank, our commitment to the well-being of the 46 million people who call rural America their home extends to our boots-on-the-ground work with municipalities, water districts and cooperatives all across the country.

As a member of the Farm Credit System, we're equiped to support you with access to credit, financing and other financial solutions to plan an execture water and waste disposal infrastructure projects to meet the needs of rural communities you serve.

How Cobank Supports Rural Water Access

Many rural water systems prefer lease agreements in order to match payments to the life expectancy of assets and more effectively manage cash flow. Through our partnership with the Nationsl Rural Water Association, your municipalities or water districts can lease water meters and other essential equipment.
